Investigating Hotel Booking Demand¶

by Khaled Salem¶

Investigation Overview¶

This presentation provides an insight of the tourism and hospitality industry by investigating hotel bookings demand globally. The aim of this presentation is to present the main findings through intuitive and easy-to-follow visualizations.

Dataset Overview¶

This data set contains booking information for a city hotel and a resort hotel made worldwide, and includes information such as when the booking was made, duration of stay, the number of adults, children, and/or babies, and the number of available parking spaces, among other things from 2015 to 2017.

Payment policies set by hotels can impact cancellation rate¶

An unusual correlation was discovered, in that almost all bookings at hotels that do not offer a refund (full payment is made prior to arrival) got cancelled indicating that adopting a no deposit policy may lead to lower cancellation rates
